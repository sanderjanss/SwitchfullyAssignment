How to build a website in 7 easy steps!

Since I've had my share of trouble creating websites, I thought it would be a good idea to write my blog about exactly that!

1. Gather Information

The first step is all about understanding your client. Define the big WHO and WHAT questions. Get to know who the site is for. Who is your target audience. What are the purposes and main goals of your site. What do you want your website personality to be. Make sure you have a clear answer to these questions to start off your site in the right direction!

2. Plan

Gather all the information provided by the client and start planning out the sitemap. This is a representation of how the different pages will be related to each other, it helps give the webdesigner a clear idea of the website's architecture!

3. Sketch

This is where your creativity is put to the test. Visualize the content, think about the components and the layout patterns you will be using. Sketch out the global layout of how you want your page to look. This shouldn't be anything close to perfection yet since you will be changing this up a lot along the way of creating your website. Keep in mind that it is the content that should lead the design, and not the other way around!

4. Content creation

Usually the client will deliver the bulk of the content, but don't underestimate your own role in this process. Provide the client with relevant, informative and rich keywords to boost a site's visibility for search engines. A process called Search Engine Optimization (SEO)!

5. Build and design

At this step, you can finally start creating. This is where all the former steps are being brought together. Use all the gathered information, content, thought out plans and sketches to design and build the website. Shape up the design by existing branding elements, logos, color choices, images as provided by the client. Good visual design is key to the site's success!

6. Test, Optimize and Launch

Check, check and triple-check your website. Test every link, test every button, optimize your images in terms of dimensions and size. Make sure the website loads properly on desktop, tablet and mobile. And don't forget to test how it runs on the major browsers!


Once everything fits into place and you have thoroughly tested your website, then it is time to upload your project to a hosting platform for the long anticipated launch!

7. Maintain and Update

Last but not least, maintain the website content over time. Important to keep in mind, that it is not enough to just create the website and be done with it. The client will after all come back to you in case of trouble or when he simply wants to add a new feature!
